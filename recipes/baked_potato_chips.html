Baked Potato Chips

Homemade baked potato chips with only 3 ingredients. Easy and straightforward recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Yukon gold potatoes
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 200 deg celcius
  2. slice potatoes to 1/8 inch thick and place on paper towel lined baking sheets
  3. Place a layer of paper towels over slices, let stand minutes
  4. Line baking sheet with parchment paper. Divide potatoes between baking sheets in an even layer
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes per side
  6. Remove from oven and cool 5 minutes before serving
