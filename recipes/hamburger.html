Tasty BBQ Hamburger

A great burger for grilling any time of the year, and very easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Beef: 1 1/2lbs lean ground beef
  • Onion: one finely chopped onion
  • Cheese: shredded Swiss or Cheddar
  • Egg: one egg for moisture and binging
  • Seasonings: fresh garlic, garlic powder, soy sauce, salt, pepper

How to make these tasty burgers

  1. Combine all ingredients into a bowl
  2. Form into four patties
  3. Fire up the bbq and grill until juices run clear and no pink meat shows approximatley 4 to 5 minutes per side
